Scheduled Water Interruption

August 23, 2019 in Rose St., Barangay Ula

Posted Date:       08/20/2019

SET 1

WHEN:

Friday, August 23, 2019 from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (8 hours)

WHERE:

Rose Street in Brgy. Ula

WHY:

Water supply has to be temporarily shut off to give way to the tapping of a newly installed pipeline to DCWD’s existing pipeline along the affected area as part of the pipeline expansion/improvement of Riverside Water Supply System.

Notes:

1) Benefit of the service improvement project: Improve water pressure of affected area and its immediate environs

2) The would-be affected customers are advised to store water in clean and covered containers at least two days before schedule.

3) Water supply may be restored earlier if works go smoothly or later if unforeseen problems arise.

4) DCWD conducts line flushing after service works are completed. However, once water is restored, customers are advised to open all faucets and allow water to run for at least two minutes. Do not drink tap water immediately but if needed, boil water beforehand as safety precaution.

5) This information is disseminated through print and broadcast media, DCWD website (www.davao-water.gov.ph), official Facebook page (www.facebook.com/davaowater) and telephone voicemail 235-3293 (DCWD).
